How To Make Cupcakes!

Follow this recipe to create a simple cupcake base. Decorate however you like!

Makes 12 delicious cupcakes!

Ingredients

  • 125g butter, softened
  • 125g caster sugar
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 125g self-raising flour
  • 2 tbsp milk

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 190/gas mark 5. Beat together the butter and sugar in a bowl until light and fluffy. Next add the eggs, flour and milk to the bowl and beat until the mixture is smooth.
  2. Divide the mixture between the paper cases and bake in the centre of the oven until the cakes have risen and are just firm to the touch in the centre. This will take about 12 to 15 minutes.
  3. Remove the cakes from the oven and leave to cool on a wire rack.

Decorate and enjoy!!
